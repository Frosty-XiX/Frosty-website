Ayushman Bharat – PM-JAY

Free Healthcare Coverage for Indian Families

What is Ayushman Bharat?

Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) is a government scheme that provides ₹5 lakh per year per family for hospitalization and healthcare services.

Key Benefits

  • Health insurance of ₹5 lakh per family per year
  • Cashless and paperless treatment
  • Pre- and post-hospitalization coverage
  • Coverage for all pre-existing conditions
  • Access to empaneled private and government hospitals across India

Eligibility Criteria

For Rural Families:

  • Living in one-room kucha house
  • No adult member (16–59 years)
  • Female-headed household with no adult male
  • SC/ST households
  • Landless laborers
  • Disabled member with no support

For Urban Families:

  • Ragpickers, domestic workers
  • Construction and manual laborers
  • Street vendors, cobblers, plumbers, painters
  • Sanitation workers, security guards, etc.

How to Check Eligibility

  1. Visit the official website: https://pmjay.gov.in
  2. Click on "Am I Eligible?"
  3. Enter your mobile number and OTP
  4. Search by name, ration card, or mobile number
